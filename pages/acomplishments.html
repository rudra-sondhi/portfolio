Charity
In the summer of 2020, me and my friend saw a rising problem in long term care homes for COVID-19 patients. Because of this, we got together and started a fundraising campaign, to help equip the long term care home with the required materials to ensure the safety of the staff and the patients. Due to this, we were able to raise $750; which is an everlasting accomplishment I possess as it helped other people survive and thrive.
